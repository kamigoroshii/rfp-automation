Pune Smart City Corporation invites bids for supply, installation, testing, and commissioning of 500 Smart LED Street Lights with wireless control system across major arterial roads under the Smart Cities Mission.

Smart Control System (NEMA Based LoRaWAN)

  • Communication Protocol: LoRaWAN Class C
  • NEMA Socket: 7-pin NEMA compliant receptacle
  • Dimming Capability: 0-100% (10% increments)
  • Scheduling: Programmable on/off times, dimming profiles
  • Energy Monitoring: Real-time power consumption tracking
  • Fault Detection: Automatic alerts for malfunctions
  • Geolocation: GPS coordinates for each luminaire
  • Gateway: LoRaWAN gateway for every 50 lights
  • Central Management System: Web-based dashboard with mobile app

Scope of Work

  • Supply of 500 LED street lights with smart controllers
  • Supply of 10 LoRaWAN gateways
  • Installation on existing poles (civil work excluded)
  • Cable laying and electrical connections
  • Testing and commissioning of complete system
  • Integration with central monitoring system
  • Training for 10 municipal staff members
  • 5 years comprehensive maintenance (AMC)

Testing Requirements

  • LM-79: Photometric testing
  • LM-80: LED lumen maintenance testing
  • IP Testing: Ingress protection verification (IP66)
  • Surge Test: 10kV as per IEC 61643-11
  • Vibration Test: As per IEC 60068-2-6
  • Salt Spray Test: 200 hours as per IEC 60068-2-11
  • LoRaWAN Certification: LoRa Alliance certified
