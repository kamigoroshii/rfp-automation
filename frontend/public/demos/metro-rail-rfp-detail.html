The Delhi Metro Rail Corporation (DMRC) invites bids from qualified manufacturers for the supply of high-quality 33kV XLPE Underground Cables for Phase IV expansion project connecting Dwarka to Greater Noida. This is a mission-critical infrastructure project requiring immediate delivery and strict adherence to international quality standards.

Technical Specifications

Parameter Specification Compliance Standard
Voltage Rating 33kV (38.5kV max) IEC 60502-2
Conductor Material Copper (99.97% purity) IS 8130
Conductor Size 240 sq.mm (Class 2 Stranded) IEC 60228
Core Configuration 3-Core Circular -
Insulation Material XLPE (Cross-linked Polyethylene) IEC 60502-2
Insulation Thickness 6.5mm minimum IEC 60502-2
Screen Copper Wire Screen -
Armour Double Steel Wire Armour (SWA) IS 3975
Outer Sheath FRLS PVC (Fire Retardant Low Smoke) IS 1554 Part 1
Temperature Rating 90°C Continuous, 250°C Short Circuit IEC 60502-2
Total Quantity 25,000 meters (25 km) -
Delivery Schedule 3 installments over 60 days -

Testing Requirements

  • Type Tests (as per IEC 60502-2): Required from NABL accredited lab (within last 5 years)
    • High Voltage Test
    • Partial Discharge Test
    • Hot Set Test
    • Flame Retardance Test
    • Impulse Voltage Test
  • Routine Tests (100% production):
    • Conductor Resistance Test
    • High Voltage Test at 70kV for 5 minutes
    • Partial Discharge Test (< 10pC at 1.5U₀)
    • Dimensional Check
  • Sample Tests: 1% of total drums for accelerated aging tests
  • Witness Testing: DMRC representatives will witness final testing at manufacturer's works

Eligibility Criteria

  • Manufacturer must have minimum 10 years experience in HV cable manufacturing
  • Annual turnover ≥ ₹100 Crores in last 3 financial years
  • Must have supplied similar cables to at least 2 govt. projects in last 5 years
  • ISO 9001:2015 certification mandatory
  • BIS license (IS 7098) required
  • Production capacity: ≥ 50 km of 33kV cables per annum
  • NABL accredited in-house testing laboratory
