☀️ Supply of 540Wp Mono-PERC Solar Modules

RFP-2026-002 | Published: December 1, 2025

Project Overview

Client Organization
Solar Energy Corporation of India
Project Value
₹120 Crores
Submission Deadline
Jan 15, 2026 12:00 PM
Project Type
Government - Renewable Energy

SECI invites bids for procurement of 10,000 units of high-efficiency Mono PERC Solar PV Modules (≥540Wp) for the development of a 50MW Solar Park in Rajasthan under the CPSU Scheme Phase II.

Technical Specifications

Parameter Specification Compliance
Module Type Monocrystalline PERC Half-Cut IEC 61215
Power Output ≥540 Wp (at STC) ±3% tolerance
Module Efficiency ≥21% IEC 61215
Cell Technology Mono PERC Half-Cut (144 cells) -
Dimensions 2278 × 1134 × 35 mm (±2mm) -
Weight ≤28.5 kg per module -
Front Glass 3.2mm AR coated tempered glass IEC 61730
Backsheet White TPO/TPT composite -
Frame Anodized aluminum alloy -
Junction Box IP68 rated with MC4 connectors IEC 61730
Performance Warranty 25 years linear (80% at year 25) -
Product Warranty 12 years -
BIS Certification Mandatory (IS 14286:2019) BIS
Total Quantity 10,000 modules (92.59 MW capacity) -

Testing & Certification

  • IEC 61215: Design Qualification and Type Approval
  • IEC 61730: Module Safety Qualification
  • IEC 61701: Salt Mist Corrosion Test
  • IEC 62716: Ammonia Corrosion Test
  • IS 14286:2019: BIS certification mandatory
  • EL Test: 100% Electroluminescence testing
  • Flash Test: 100% modules at manufacturer's facility
  • PID Test: Potential Induced Degradation resistance
  • LID Test: Light Induced Degradation ≤2%

Eligibility Criteria

  • Manufacturer must be on ALMM (Approved List of Models and Manufacturers)
  • Annual production capacity ≥500 MW
  • Average annual turnover ≥₹200 Crores in last 3 years
  • Experience of supplying ≥25 MW modules to govt./PSU in last 5 years
  • ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001 certifications required
  • In-house testing lab with IEC accreditation
  • Must have manufacturing facility in India (Domestic Content Requirement)
